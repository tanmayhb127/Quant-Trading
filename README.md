# Nifty 50 Trade Setups Scraper

Comprehensive analysis of Nifty 50 trade setup predictions from 10 major Indian financial news sources.

## Sources
- Bloomberg
- CNBC-TV18
- ET Now
- Mint
- NDTV Profit
- Times Now Business
- India Today Business
- Money Control
- Financial Express
- Economic Times Live

## Project Goal
Compare accuracy of news sources' predicted support/resistance ranges against actual market High/Low prices.
